About Common Thread Vision

Building Bridges in Glasgow's Communities

Common Thread Vision (CTV) is a registered Scottish charity dedicated to creating inclusive spaces where community members can share their journey to UK citizenship, celebrate cultural diversity, and contribute to the rich tapestry of British and Scottish culture.

  • Supporting integration through cultural exchange and community activities

  • Providing platforms for sharing citizenship journey experiences

  • Creating safe spaces for community development and learning

Common Questions

Supporting Your Integration Journey

We're here to support community members at every step of their journey. Whether you're working towards citizenship or looking to connect with your local community, we provide the resources and support you need.

Contact Us for Support

How can I get involved with Common Thread Vision?

You can join our community by attending our events, participating in our cultural exchange programs, or volunteering. Contact us to learn about upcoming opportunities.

What support do you offer for citizenship journey?

We provide a platform for sharing experiences, learning from others who have been through the process, and accessing resources to support your citizenship journey.

Where are your activities located?

Our activities are centered in Glasgow, with our main location at St Andrews Crescent. We ensure our venues are easily accessible by public transport.

How can I access your services?

Our services are open to all community members. Simply contact us or attend one of our events to get started. We welcome everyone interested in cultural exchange and community development.

Common Thread Vision supports community integration and cultural exchange in Glasgow, helping build stronger, more inclusive communities.
