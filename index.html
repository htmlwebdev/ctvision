Welcome to Common Thread Vision

Supporting integration and community development in Glasgow

We help community members develop strong connections, share experiences of their citizenship journeys, and contribute to the rich tapestry of British and Scottish culture. Our impact focuses on creating inclusive spaces where people can learn, grow, and build stronger communities together.
